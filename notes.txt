HOW TO DROP IN YOUR OWN PHOTOGRAPHY
  Every artwork is a <div class="art ..."> with an empty <div class="surface"></div>.
  Two ways to use a real image:

  1) Easiest — set a background on the surface:
     <div class="surface" style="background-image:url('img/your-photo.jpg')"></div>

  2) Or replace the surface with an <img>:
     <div class="art ..."><img src="img/your-photo.jpg" alt="">...</div>

  The soft-focus/film overlay sits on top automatically, so your photos
  inherit the dreamy Petra-Collins wash. Remove ".art .surface{filter:...}"
  in the <style> if you want them perfectly sharp.
