The agency

About ÉTHÉRÉE

A boutique model management house built on tenderness, taste, and the belief that softness is a kind of strength.

Our story

Founded on a feeling.

ÉTHÉRÉE began as a small studio in Paris with a simple idea — that the most memorable images are the gentle ones. We represent talent the way we'd want to be represented: with patience, honesty, and genuine love for the craft.

Today we manage faces across twelve cities, working with the houses, magazines and beauty brands who share our quiet, romantic sensibility.
